Koi Princess Slot — Overview

Koi Princess is a 5×3 video slot from NetEnt, released in November 2015 and built around a calm Japanese koi-pond aesthetic with anime-style art. Players often look for it as koi princess slot, koi princess demo or koi princess RTP — and the appeal is the same in every search: a feature-rich game with a relaxed visual tone. The reels are framed by lanterns, falling petals and gentle water effects, while traditional Japanese instruments and soft percussion play in the background.

The slot runs on 20 fixed paylines and pays from left to right starting on reel 1. Its RTP is 96.23%, volatility is medium, and the max win is 1,000× the stake. What makes Koi Princess stand out is the bonus structure: four random features can fire on any spin, and four main bonus features can be triggered through scatter symbols. There is also an optional Bonus Bet that doubles the spin cost in exchange for a higher chance of triggering features.

How to Play Koi Princess

The flow is straightforward. You set a stake, spin the reels, and watch how symbols line up across the 20 fixed paylines from left to right. There is no need to choose how many lines to activate — every spin always covers all 20.

  • Coin value: sets how much each coin is worth. Adjust this to scale the spin cost up or down.
  • Bet level: sets how many coins are wagered per line. Higher bet level = larger stake and bigger potential wins.
  • Spin / Auto / Quick spin: manual spins, autoplay with limits, or a faster spin mode for experienced players.
  • Bonus Bet: optional toggle that doubles the cost of each spin and increases the chance of triggering features (more on this below).

Wins on Koi Princess come from three places: matching symbols on a payline, random features that fire without warning, and the four main bonus rounds activated by scatter symbols. Knowing what to expect from each makes the session easier to follow.

Symbols You'll Encounter

The paytable is split into card symbols (low pay) and Asian-themed icons (high pay), plus a wild and a scatter.

  • Low-paying symbols: stylised playing-card values 10, J, Q, K and A. They appear most often and pay smaller amounts.
  • High-paying symbols: Feng Shui coins, the lion, the dragon and the Koi Princess herself. The Princess is the top regular symbol.
  • Wild: the Great Wave wild substitutes for all regular symbols (it does not replace the scatter) and can deliver some of the largest base-game line wins.
  • Scatter: the dragon "BONUS" scatter — landing three of these on reels 1, 3 and 5 triggers one of the four main bonus features.

Bonus Features in Detail

Koi Princess is unusually loaded with features for a 2015 NetEnt slot. There are four random features that can hit on any spin and four main bonus features triggered by scatters.

Random features (any spin)

  • 5-Hit: guarantees a 5-of-a-kind win across one of the medium-paying symbols.
  • Random Wilds: drops between 4 and 9 overlay wild symbols onto the reels before they resolve.
  • Wild Reels: turns 2 to 5 entire reels fully wild for a single, very high-paying spin.
  • Bonus Activation: triggers one of the main bonus features outright, even without scatter symbols on screen.

Main bonus features (triggered by 3 scatters)

  • Sure Win Free Spins: 10 free spins where every spin is guaranteed to produce a winning combination.
  • Wild Reels Free Spins: 10 free spins, each with at least one fully wild reel for bigger combinations.
  • Coin Win: an instant cash prize awarded as a multiplier of the current stake.
  • Bonus Wheel: a multi-tier wheel that can award stake multipliers, free spins, or step you up to a higher tier of the wheel.

Bonus Bet — How It Works

The Bonus Bet is one of the most distinctive things about Koi Princess. Switch it on and every spin costs twice as much, but two things change:

  • The probability of triggering a random feature roughly doubles.
  • The probability of landing the main bonus features via scatters increases noticeably.

The published RTP of 96.23% applies in the standard mode. Activating the Bonus Bet shifts the RTP slightly, and reviewers usually treat it as a "spice level" choice: more frequent features in exchange for a faster bankroll burn. If you're testing the game in demo mode, try a few sessions both with and without Bonus Bet to see which pacing fits you.

RTP, Volatility & Max Win

RTP (Return to Player) is a theoretical long-term return across millions of spins — not a promise for any single session. Koi Princess sits at 96.23%, which is right around the industry average for NetEnt slots. Volatility describes how wins are distributed: Koi Princess is rated medium, meaning you get a steady stream of small-to-medium hits with occasional larger bursts from the random features and bonus rounds.

The maximum win is 1,000× the stake, typically reached through Wild Reels Free Spins or top-tier Bonus Wheel multipliers. It's a sensible cap for a medium-volatility game — high enough to feel rewarding, but without the brutal swings of a 10,000×+ slot.

Strategy & Tips

Slots are random — there is no system that beats the math. But there are sensible habits that make a Koi Princess session more enjoyable and easier to manage.

  • Try the demo first. Five minutes in demo mode is enough to understand the four random features, the four bonus rounds, and the Bonus Wheel branching. Real money should never be your first lesson.
  • Set a session bankroll. Decide upfront how much you're willing to spend, and stop when you hit that number — win or lose.
  • Pick a bet size you can sustain. A reasonable rule of thumb is 100+ spins worth of stake in your bankroll. That gives features room to trigger.
  • Understand the Bonus Bet trade-off. Doubling the spin cost roughly doubles your chance of features. It's not "free value" — it's a pacing choice.
  • Don't chase losses. If a session goes cold, walk away. The math doesn't owe you anything for previous spins.
  • Use casino tools. Most licensed casinos let you set deposit limits, loss limits, session time-outs and self-exclusion. Use them proactively, not after a problem.

Mobile Play & Tips

Koi Princess runs in HTML5 directly in the browser, so iOS and Android phones, tablets and desktops all work without any app install. Performance is smooth on modern devices, and the layout adapts to portrait and landscape.

  • Use stable Wi-Fi or a strong mobile signal — interrupted sessions can be frustrating if a feature is mid-trigger.
  • Quick-spin and autoplay increase the pace dramatically — stay aware of how fast money moves through the session.
  • If your casino offers session-time and budget limits, set them before you start playing.
